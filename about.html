Our approach

Built to be straightforward.

Exterior projects can involve multiple systems, hidden conditions and a lot of decisions. The goal is to make those decisions easier by walking the project, identifying priorities and defining the work clearly.

“The details between trades matter just as much as the material itself.”

Roofing should meet siding cleanly. Windows and doors should integrate with the weather barrier. Framing should provide a sound base for every finish that follows. That whole-system thinking is the foundation of the Storm Proof brand.
